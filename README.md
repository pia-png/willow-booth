# WILLOW BOOTH

Willow Booth is a web-based photo booth where users can capture memories through three nature-inspired photo strips: **Woods**, **Ocean**, and **Sky**. I built it with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The project uses the browser's webcam to create themed photo strips that users can instantly download.

---

## Features

* Three photo strips: **Woods**, **Ocean**, and **Sky**
* Live webcam preview using 
* Animated 3-second countdown before each photo
* Download your completed photo strip as an image
* Made with custom font: willow

---

## Built With

* HTML5
* CSS3
* JavaScript 

---


## How It Works

1. Choose one of three nature-inspired themes.
2. View yourself in the live camera preview.
3. Pose while the countdown begins.
4. Capture your photos.
5. Your photo strip is ready automatically.
6. Download your finished creation with one click!
7. You get to keep your memories digitally in a cute way

## Why I Made This

One of my favorite things to do with friends and families is to go to photo booths at malls! I wanted to create a fun way to do it at home with some designs that I wanted to create. From the digital photo booths I've done, I haven't really seen nature based photo strips, so I wanted to try that out + add my original font.
